Empowering Rural Entrepreneurs Through BRUPY

Are you a young entrepreneur from a backward or underdeveloped region seeking to establish or expand your business? Subsidy Mantra* offers expert consultancy to help you leverage the *Backward and Regional Upliftment Programme for Youth (BRUPY) — a government initiative designed to foster inclusive economic development through entrepreneurship and financial support.

About BRUPY

The *Backward and Regional Upliftment Programme for Youth (BRUPY)* is a government-backed scheme aimed at empowering youth in underserved and rural areas. It promotes entrepreneurship by offering financial incentives, capacity-building programs, and sustained support for setting up viable business enterprises.

Key Features

✅ Capital subsidies for machinery and equipment

✅ Soft loans and financial assistance for new ventures

✅ Support for setting up micro and small enterprises

✅ Skill development and entrepreneurship training

✅ Comprehensive handholding from ideation to execution

✅ Interest Subsidy* – 6% to 9% based on loan amount (up to ₹10 crore)

✅ CGTMSE Fee Reimbursement – Government covers guarantee fee for collateral-free loans

Eligibility Overview

•⁠ ⁠Individuals aged 18–45 residing in notified backward or regional areas

•⁠ ⁠Those intending to start or expand small to medium-scale enterprises

•⁠ ⁠Applicants with valid identification and domicile certificates

•⁠ ⁠Priority is given to SC/ST/OBC, minority, and women entrepreneurs

•⁠ ⁠Individuals not already availing benefits under similar state schemes

•⁠ ⁠Initial eligibility evaluation and strategic advisory
